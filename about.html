Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Culinary Excellence

BASORIA was founded in 2009 by Pak Bambang Suryadi, a passionate food artisan who believed that every bowl of bakso should tell a story of quality and tradition. What started as a small street-side warung has grown into a beloved brand, serving thousands of loyal customers across Indonesia.

Our name, BASORIA, comes from the Javanese phrase meaning "to serve with pride." Every meatball is hand-crafted with care, using only the finest ingredients and recipes passed down through three generations.

Bambang Suryadi, Founder

Our Vision

To become Indonesia's most beloved bakso brand, bringing the authentic taste of traditional Indonesian cuisine to every corner of the country while maintaining our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

  • Serve premium quality bakso at affordable prices
  • Maintain authentic recipes passed through generations
  • Create memorable dining experiences for every customer
  • Support local suppliers and sustainable practices
